Section 01 / The Challenge

Three Reps Buried in Tickets That Never Needed a Human

01

The company had grown from 200 to 1,400 customers in 18 months, but headcount in support hadn't kept pace. The three-person team was handling 200+ tickets per week — roughly 40% of which were variations of the same six questions. Every hour spent copy-pasting FAQ answers was an hour not spent on onboarding issues, feature complaints, and the nuanced problems that actually drive churn.

02

First response times had slipped from under 2 hours to 14 hours. Customer satisfaction scores were falling. Two of the three reps had flagged burnout. The team lead was spending more time managing queue backlogs than managing the team.

03

The root cause wasn't volume — it was that there was no filter. Every ticket, regardless of complexity, landed in the same queue and required the same manual triage, response-drafting, and follow-up process.

Section 02 / The Solution

AI Triage, Auto-Resolution, and Smart Escalation — in One System

01

We built a three-layer system: an AI classifier that categorises every incoming ticket by intent and complexity, an auto-responder that handles Tier 1 queries end-to-end, and an escalation engine that routes complex tickets directly to the right rep with full context pre-loaded.

02

For the 72% of tickets that are Tier 1 — FAQs, password resets, billing lookups, basic how-tos — the system resolves them within 60 seconds using verified answer templates, CRM data lookups, and dynamic personalisation. No human involved. Customer gets an accurate, branded response.

03

For the remaining tickets, the system doesn't just forward them — it classifies the issue, summarises the customer's history, flags sentiment, and assigns to the correct specialist. The rep opens a ticket already knowing what's happening.

Section 03 / The Process

How We Built It

Step 01

Audited the Ticket History

Analysed 3 months of historical tickets to identify intent categories, resolution patterns, and the exact queries that could be safely automated — no guesswork.

Step 02

Built the Intent Classifier

Trained a classification layer on the company's own ticket data to route by intent and confidence score — distinguishing between what can be automated and what needs a human.

Step 03

Designed Auto-Response Templates

Wrote and connected 24 auto-response flows covering the top Tier 1 categories, each personalised with CRM data (account name, plan type, recent activity).

Step 04

Integrated the Escalation Engine

Connected the classifier to the helpdesk's assignment rules — high-complexity or low-confidence tickets route to a named rep with a pre-generated context brief.

Step 05

Deployed & Monitored

Ran a 3-week shadow period where all AI responses were reviewed before sending. Once accuracy hit 96%, switched to fully autonomous Tier 1 resolution.

Section 04 / The Results

A Support Function That Scales Without Headcount

01

Within 90 days of go-live, the team's ticket queue had dropped by 72% — not because there were fewer customers, but because the majority of inbound volume was now being resolved without reaching a human. First response time dropped from 14 hours to under 90 seconds for Tier 1 queries.

02

The reps' experience changed fundamentally. Instead of queue management, they were doing actual support work — solving edge cases, nurturing at-risk accounts, contributing to product feedback loops. CSAT scores recovered within 6 weeks and have since hit their highest-ever level.

03

The company added 600 more customers the following quarter with no additional support hire. The system scaled seamlessly, handling the increased volume at the same resolution rate.
